 SEO	Let search engines know what to index and what not to
 Private Admin Routes	Prevent sensitive pages (like /admin) from being crawled
 Prevent Crawl Overload	Block bots from crawling heavy or unnecessary routes
 Staging or Dev Environments	Block bots from indexing non-production sites
 Social Sharing	Allow Twitter, Facebook, LinkedIn bots to generate previews correctly
